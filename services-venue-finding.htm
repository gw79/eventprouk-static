Free Venue Finding Service

We know that trying to find the right venue can be time-consuming, expensive and often frustrating. It’s our job to save you time and money.

From 5* luxury to functional budget accommodation, intimate meeting rooms for two people to a large-scale event for 2,000 delegates, a surprise party to a promising product launch, an awards ceremony to a fashion show, we are a one stop shop for sourcing venues in the right place and at the right price. With access to all leading chains as well as independent and specialist venues, we offer a completely unbiased service. However large or small your requirements, we’ll find the perfect solution.

 

Keeping it real

If you’re looking for accommodation, you can count on us to explore all possibilities. We’re realistic about budgets and book single and group bedrooms on a daily basis so we can pass on discounted room rates! We are happy to source and negotiate individual and multiple bed night rates on your behalf. The more you book, the cheaper the rates will be...and as our venue-finding service really is free, you have absolutely nothing to lose!

What’s more, as we are registered TIDS/IATA travel agents, you’ll benefit from the corporate, government and charity rates that we can obtain.

We have long established relationships with a wide variety of venues throughout the UK and Europe which enable us to negotiate the best rates and add value to your meeting / event: we’re particularly proud of our elastic approach, stretching your budget as far it will go without compromising on quality.

 

Always on your side

From regular visits to the venue through to nurturing ongoing relationship with personnel there and a commitment to extending our service to include new locations, we keep your best interests at heart.
